Dynamic Bus Scheduling System

A Python-based application designed to optimize public bus transportation routes dynamically. It uses the NetworkX library for graph-based pathfinding and Matplotlib for visualization. The system intelligently finds the shortest or most efficient route between bus stops, with potential for real-time integration in smart city systems.
